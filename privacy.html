Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. Emotica is designed with a "Local-First" architecture, meaning we prioritize keeping your personal data on your device. This policy describes our commitment to your privacy and how we handle the minimal data we do interact with.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Emotica is an offline-capable application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your mood entries, journal notes, tags, or personal patterns on any external servers. All information you enter is stored exclusively in a secure SQLite database on your physical device.

2. Local Data Usage

The information you provide is used strictly to provide the following local functionalities:

  • Generating mood trend charts and insights.
  • Maintaining your emotional timeline.
  • Triggering local habit reminders/notifications.
  • Exporting your data into PDF or Excel formats.

3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Mixpanel) that monitor your personal activity inside the app. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

4. Haptic Feedback and Notifications

The app may use your device's vibration motor and notification services. These are handled locally by the Android operating system and do not involve data transmission.

5. Data Deletion and Portability

You have sovereign control over your data. You can delete individual entries or clear all application data at any time through the Settings menu. Please note that once data is deleted locally, it is unrecoverable as we do not keep backups.

6. Compliance with Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

As we do not collect any personal information on our servers, we are in full compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

7. Contacting Us

If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us at support@theaa.com.
