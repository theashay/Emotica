Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. Emotica is designed with a "Local-First" architecture, meaning we prioritize keeping your personal data on your device.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect or store your mood entries, journal notes, or profile information on our servers. All data you enter into Emotica is stored locally on your device's internal storage.

2. Data Usage

The information you provide is used solely to generate your personal experience within the app, such as calculating mood trends, displaying your journey timeline, and triggering reminders.

3. Third-Party Services

Emotica does not use third-party analytics or tracking tools that monitor your personal activity inside the app. If you use the PDF export feature, the document is generated locally.

4. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual entries or clear all data from the settings menu. Deleting the application will also remove all locally stored data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
